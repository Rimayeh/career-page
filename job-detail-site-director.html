Site Director

Job Description

Key Responsibilities

Sales & Business Development

  • Develop and execute sales strategies to drive revenue and market growth.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients, partners, and stakeholders.
  • Identify new business opportunities and market trends.
  • Align sales goals with company objectives through internal collaboration.

Operations & Site Management

  • Oversee daily site operations to ensure efficiency and productivity.
  • Implement operational policies and monitor KPIs and financial metrics.
  • Ensure regulatory compliance and adherence to company policies.
  • Manage collaboration between sales, operations, and technical teams.
  • Experience with sheet metal operations and contact management preferred.

International Coordination

  • Act as the main liaison between the Arizona site and Taiwan headquarters.
  • Participate in executive meetings, adjusting to Taiwan’s time zone as needed.
  • Provide regular site performance updates and reports.
  • Promote cross-cultural communication and alignment across teams.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in sales, operations, or site management.
  • Fluent in Mandarin and English (spoken and written).
  • Strong leadership and interpersonal skills, with proven ability to collaborate across diverse teams and coordinate international operations.
  • Flexible with working hours to accommodate overseas meetings.
  • Solid knowledge of business operations, financial planning, and cost management.
  • Familiarity with manufacturing technologies; experience in the sheet metal industry is a plus
